Data

On the 23rd of March 2020, the UK was put on lockdown to contain the spread of COVID-19. We used several open source datasets to document the effect of this lockdown on bee habitats across the UK and Ireland by collecting data from the 1st of January 2020 until the 27th of May 2020.
