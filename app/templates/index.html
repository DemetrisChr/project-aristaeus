Welcome to Project Aristaeus!

Bees are crucial to ecosystems: they guarantee the pollination of many cultivated and wild plants. One third of the worldâ€™s food production is dependent on pollination by bees and other insects. In the UK and Ireland, the population of wild bees is declining at an alarming rate. However, the lockdown measures due to COVID-19 have quieted the planet and given bees a moment of respite.

The challenge

Project Aristaeus was created in response to the Quiet Planet Space Apps COVID-19 Challenge, organized by NASA, ESA, CSA, CNES, and JAXA. The team was tasked with looking into the global and local environmental changes caused by COVID-19 and the associated societal responses.

The solution

Project Aristaeus aims to show the effect of COVID-19 lockdown measures on bee habitats and raise awareness about endangered bee populations. We provide the first website which uses space-based environmental data to monitor geographically where bee populations can thrive in the UK and Ireland. By analyzing climate data, Project Aristaeus quickly compares and evaluates changes in environmental factors and their effect on bee habitats.
